SAI BATTERY

automotive battery or car battery is a rechargeable battery that is used to start a motor vehicle.
Its main purpose is to provide an electric current to the electric-powered starting motor,
which in turn starts the chemically-powered internal combustion engine that actually propels the vehicle.
